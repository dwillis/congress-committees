Committee Timelines

Pick a committee to see everyone who joined or left it, Congress by Congress, since 1993. Each row is one Congress; the cluster in January is the organizing-day roster, when a whole party's slate of committee members is elected at once. A dot standing alone mid-year is usually a resignation or a single new appointment. Hover a dot for details, click it to open the original resolution or Congressional Record letter.
